North, VA locals startled by spotting of ominous local legend "The Hoochler"

By Dent Head • March 17, 2026

Residents of North, Virginia were left shaken late Monday evening after multiple witnesses reported seeing what they describe as the elusive and ominous figure known only as "The Hoochler."

The creature, long considered a local legend, is said to roam wooded areas at dusk. Eyewitness accounts describe a tall, shadowy figure moving silently between trees.

Authorities have not confirmed the reports, but locals remain on edge as the story spreads.
